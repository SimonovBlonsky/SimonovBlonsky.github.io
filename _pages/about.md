I currently work as a research assistant in **State Key Lab of CAD & CG**, College of Computer Science and Technology, <a href='https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/'>Zhejiang University (ZJU)</a>, under supervision of Researcher <a href='http://www.cad.zju.edu.cn/home/zhpcui/'>Zhaopeng Cui</a>.

I obtained my ME degree of civil engineering in Zhejiang University in 2024, from the department of Smart Construction and Construction Management. During my master's studies, I developed a strong interest in robotics, SLAM, and 3D vision. This passion led me to explore further in-depth research in these areas during my future PhD. Before joining ZJU, I obtained my Bachelor degree of construction management in <a href='https://www.scut.edu.cn/en/'>South China University of Technology</a> in 2021.

My current research centers on **Localization** and **Navigation**, especially in Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (**SLAM**), Visual-Inertial Odometry (**VIO**) and **Learning-based** navigation.
