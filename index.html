About Me

I currently work as a research assistant at the State Key Laboratory of CAD&CG, College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of researcher Zhaopeng Cui.

I obtained my Master's degree in Civil Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2024, from the Department of Smart Construction and Construction Management. During my graduate studies, I developed a strong passion for Robotics, SLAM, and 3D vision, which has driven my pursuit of deeper research in these areas as I prepare for my PhD. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Construction Management from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2021.

My current research primarily focuses on Localization and Navigation, with an emphasis on Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), Visual-Inertial Odometry (VIO), and Learning-based Navigation.
