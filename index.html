About Me

I am a research assistant at the State Key Laboratory of CAD&CG, College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Prof. Zhaopeng Cui in the ZJU 3D Vision Group. My research focuses on learning-based Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), Visual & LiDAR-inertial Odometry (VIO & LIO), and 3D computer vision.

I obtained my M.E. degree in Smart Construction from Zhejiang University in 2024, supervised by Prof. Zhenfen Jin and Prof. Jiangpeng Shu. Before my time at ZJU, I obtained my B.E. degree from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2021.

Now I am seeking full-time Ph.D. opportunities in Robotics and 3D computer vision.
