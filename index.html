Chen Guyuan

I currently work as a research assistant in State Key Lab of CAD & CG, College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, under supervision of Prof. Zhaopeng Cui in the ZJU 3D Vision Group. I obtained my M.Eng degree in smart construction from Zhejiang University in 2024, supervised by Prof. Zhenfen Jin and Prof. Jiangpeng Shu. Prior to that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree from South China University of Technology in 2021.

My current research centers on Robotics and 3D computer vision, especially in Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM).
