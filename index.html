About Me

I currently work as a research assistant at the State Key Laboratory of CAD&CG, College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Dr. Zhaopeng Cui in ZJU 3D Vision group.

I obtained my M.E. degree in Civil Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2024, supervised by Senior Engineer Zhenfen Jin and co-supervised by Dr. Jiangpeng Shu in the Department of Smart Construction and Construction Management, where my research focused on robotics in smart construction, sensor fusion-based measurement and building information modeling (BIM).

During my graduate studies, I was involved in multiple projects related to SLAM, Robotics and 3D Vision, which developed my passion for these fields. Driven by my interest in further exploration, I joined the ZJU 3D Vision group as a research assistant after completing my M.E. degree, preparing for my Ph.D. studies in these areas. Before my time at ZJU, I obtained my B.E. degree in Construction Management from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2021.

My research primarily focuses on Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), Visual & LiDAR-inertial Odometry (VIO & LIO), and 3D reconstruction.
Currently, I am doing research on Large Language Models (LLM) and Reinforcement Learning (RL) in robot localization and navigation.

Now I am seeking full-time research assistant and Ph.D. opportunities in Robotics.
