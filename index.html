About Me

Hi! I'm Guyuan Chen. I currently work as a research assistant in State Key Lab of CAD & CG, College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), under supervision of Researcher Zhaopeng Cui.

I obtained my ME degree of civil engineering in Zhejiang University in 2024, from the department of Smart Construction and Construction Management. During my master's studies, I developed a strong interest in robotics, SLAM, and 3D vision. This passion led me to explore further in-depth research in these areas during my future PhD. Before joining ZJU, I obtained my Bachelor degree of construction management in South China University of Technology in 2021.

My current research centers on Localization and Navigation, especially in Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), Visual-Inertial Odometry (VIO) and Learning-based navigation.
